University Students Bus Management System

DailyGoods is a comprehensive online shopping platform designed to offer a wide range of daily essentials under one roof. It allows users to register, log in securely, and browse products categorized by type such as groceries, beauty products, electronics, and fashion accessories. The system features dynamic product displays with images, prices, and detailed descriptions. It provides an interactive user experience with smooth navigation, product hover effects, and responsive design for all devices. The goal of DailyGoods is to simplify the shopping process and make essential items easily accessible to users anytime, anywhere.
